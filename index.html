Submitting Content to Possum Every Hour

Submissions are currently not accepted, sorry! Current focus is to get the bot and the codebase up and running on new platforms before starting to populate it with new data. Once new submittions will be accepted, an email address will be published where you can email in your pictures to submit.

All pictures used by the bot belong to their original owners. Possum Every Hour is a non-profit ran bot. The bot and any of it's social media profiles are not for sale and never will be. Created by @ThunderySteak. Website source code available at Github.
